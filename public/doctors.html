Link Your Bottle



Our Story:

We are simple. Prescription bottle technology has been proven to improve patient outcomes. Better support groups has been proven to improve patient outcomes. We are combining those two principles into one product.

Why the device works

Your device registers each time you take a pill, without you doing anything.

  • The device automatically is linked to your health record, no setup required.
  • We take your family and empower them with how your medication may effect your behavior. Using weekly 30 second surveys, we let your providers know how your sleeping, eating, and mood has been based on your supporters.
  • Access what your chosen supporters, friends, or family have to say about your mood. Communicate that information with your doctor, use our guidelines to minimize unpleasant side effects through holistic methods. This way, you can start taking action before your doctors apointment.

Our Support System

  • Select One -> Five people you interact with often and like to be around, we recommend at least two.
  • Every week, on the day of their choice, your supporters will get a 30 second survey about your behavior, covering the highest behavioral risks specific to your medication. They will not know what medication you are on exactly, unless you select so.
  • The surveys are collected, anonymized, and displayed to you to see! From there, we offer a variety of actions to take on trouble sleeping, eating, and getting active.

Emergency Feature

  • We are Social Beings: when in danger, your pill bottle can act as your lifeline. By pushing a secure button on the device, your personalized emergency to your selected network of friends, family, and emergency personell.
